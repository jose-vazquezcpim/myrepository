Prompt Library for AURA ecosystem

This library organizes AURA prompts into categories to support reporting, internal communications, monitoring, and stakeholder engagement. Each category includes examples and can be expanded based on use case.

A. MBR Insights (Iris)

For VP/CVP-level audiences, focusing on MBR summaries:

  • Ask agent Iris to provide an executive summary that organizes top 5 Highlights insights and top 5 lowlights for separated by MCA, Per Seat, and Marketplace.
  • Ask agent Iris to draft a CVP-level briefing that highlights overall sentiment tone, risks, opportunities, and next steps on [launch].
  • Ask agent Iris to draft an email for CVPs summarizing the impact of [initiative] with a call to action.
  • As agent Iris to pull an executive summary of this week’s insights keyword [subject].
  • Ask agent Iris to give me a full report on [topic] across MCA per-seat.
  • Ask agent Iris What’s the overall sentiment trend for [keyword] this month?
B. Marketplace Prompts (Frida)

For Marketplace topics related with public, private, and customer support:

  • Ask Frida to Draft an email for CVPs summarizing the impact of [initiative] with a call to action.
  • Ask Frida to Summarize the top Benefits for Marketplace, particularly [keyword].
  • Highlight analyst concerns or praise around [announcement] from the last 30 days.
  • Compare analyst tone on [subject] across Gartner, Forrester, and IDC.
C. Commerce Prompts (Gibbs)

Focused on third-party insights and competitive intelligence:

  • Summarize the top 3 insights from the latest analyst reports on [launch].
  • Compare analyst tone on [subject] across Gartner, Forrester, and IDC.
  • Highlight analyst concerns or praise around [announcement] from the last 30 days.
D. Incident Management Prompts (Jasper)

For tracking press mentions and sentiment across regions:

  • Ask Jasper to List all IcMs mentioning [subject] this week, broken down by Engineering team.
  • Ask Jasper to give me Weekly ICM report.
  • Ask Jasper to provide with my ICM incidents.
  • What’s the theme for the last [] days?
